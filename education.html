The Four Pillars

Essential steps to prevent foodborne illness at home.

1
Clean

  • Wash hands for 20s with soap.
  • Wash fruits/veggies under water.
  • Sanitize surfaces daily.

2
Separate

  • Different boards for meat/veg.
  • Keep raw meat in bottom shelf.
  • Don't reuse marinade liquid.

3
Cook

  • Poultry: 165°F (74°C).
  • Ground Beef: 160°F (71°C).
  • Serve while steaming hot.

4
Chill

  • Fridge below 40°F (4°C).
  • Refrigerate within 2 hours.
  • Never thaw on the counter.

Smart Dining Guide

Choosing a Safe Establishment

When dining out, your health depends on the kitchen's hygiene. Look for these signs of quality:

  • Visible Grades: Ensure the City Health Grade is displayed.
  • Hygiene Kits: Staff should use gloves and hairnets for prep.
  • Restroom Cleanliness: Often reflects the kitchen's standard.
  • Freshness: Avoid buffets where food isn't kept at temp.
🛡️

Project Fact

Following these can reduce the risk of foodborne bacteria by up to 90%.
